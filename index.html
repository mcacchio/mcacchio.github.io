    Workin'

    • In college, I worked my way up from a delivery guy to the manager of Tiger Foods, Princeton's student-run food delivery agency.

    • After school, I joined a boutique investment banking firm called Shattuck Hammond Partner, where I worked on financial advisory engagements for not-for-profit hospitals and health systems.

      I worked there for five years, progressing from an Analyst to Associate.

      Along the way, Shattuck Hammond was acquired by Morgan Keegan, and Morgan Keegan was acquired by Raymond James. Between promotions and acquisitions, I had a lot of different business cards during my time there.

      After five years of hard work, I headed back to school to pursue my MBA and MPH degrees.
